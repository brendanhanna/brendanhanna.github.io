AI Resume Architect

A practical, modular guide for building and tailoring resumes with AI.

The Core Philosophy

The goal isn’t to let AI write your resume. The goal is to stop fighting the mechanics so you can focus on judgment, accuracy, and strategy.

1. Master Library Stop rewriting. Build one messy, giant source of truth and let AI organize it.
2. Context Engineering Never prompt without a target. Always feed the Job Description (JD) to the AI first.
3. Strategic Review Use AI as a blunt executive reviewer to find gaps you can't see yourself.
